# Creator Dashboard - Feature Guide

## Overview

The **Creator Dashboard** is a comprehensive management hub for creators to launch IP assets, post products, create discovery feed content, and track performance metrics.

**Access at:** `/creator`

---

## Dashboard Sections

### 1. Quick Action Buttons (Top)

Three primary actions for launching income streams:

#### 🚀 Launch IP

- Create and launch intellectual property as tradable tokens
- Set initial liquidity (minimum $1,000)
- Define launch duration (days before buyback enabled)
- Automatic 30% creator forfeit applied

#### 📦 Post Product

- Sell digital products (PDFs, Art, Tools)
- Set custom pricing
- Track sales and earnings

#### 💬 Create Post

- Share content to discovery feed
- Build audience and credibility
- Add tags for discoverability

## Feature Details

### 1. Launch IP (Modal)

**Step 1: Details**

- IP Title (required)
- Description (optional but recommended)
- Launch Duration in days
- Click "Next: Set Liquidity"

**Step 2: Liquidity**

- Input initial liquidity amount (minimum $1,000)
- See automatic 30% forfeit calculation
- Understand 70% creator keep ratio
- Verify initial token allocation

**Calculation Example:**

```
You invest: $10,000
30% forfeit (to protocol): -$3,000
70% you keep (liquidity pool): $7,000
Your tokens received: 7,000 @ $1/token
```

**Step 3: Review & Launch**

- Confirm all details
- Review liquidity calculations
- Click "Launch IP"
- Instant confirmation

**After Launch:**

- IP enters LAUNCH_PHASE
- Buyback disabled (trading only)
- Floor price locked
- 30% fees on all sells collected to liquidity
- You receive all earnings

---

### 2. Post Product (Modal)

**Select Product Type**

- **PDF:** eBooks, guides, reports
- **Art:** Digital art, illustrations
- **Tool:** Software, templates, tools

**Fill Product Details**

- Title (required)
- Description (what's included?)
- File name (how it's named for downloads)
- Price (USD, must be > $0)

**Preview**

- See how product appears to buyers
- Verify all information

**Post Product**

- Instant publication
- Listed in your portfolio
- Available on marketplace

---

### 3. Create Discovery Post (Modal)

**Edit View**

- **Title:** Main headline (max 120 chars)
- **Content:** Full post body (max 5,000 chars)
- **Tags:** Comma-separated topics

**Preview View**

- Live preview as seen by audience
- Shows engagement metrics (starts at 0)
- Display of all tags
- Estimated reach

**Post to Discovery**

- Visible to entire creator community
- Builds your profile and credibility
- Drives audience engagement
- Tags improve discoverability

**Best Practices:**

- Share behind-the-scenes content
- Post tips and insights
- Announce new products/IPs
- Engage with comments
- Use relevant tags

## Metrics & Tracking

### IP Metrics

- **Current Price:** Liquidity ÷ Circulating Supply
- **Holders:** Number of active token holders
- **Status:** Phase in lifecycle
- **Earnings:** Cumulative revenue

### Product Metrics

- **Sales:** Number of units sold
- **Views:** Page impressions
- **Rating:** Average 5-star rating
- **Earnings:** Revenue (Sales × Price)

### Post Metrics

- **Likes:** Heart reactions
- **Comments:** Discussion count
- **Views:** Post impressions
- **Engagement %:** (Likes + Comments) ÷ Views × 100

## Common Questions

**Q: Why 30% forfeit on IP launch?**
A: Aligns creator incentives with platform success. Creates liquidity pool security.

**Q: Can I change my IP launch after posting?**
A: Basic details yes, but liquidity is locked. Plan carefully.

**Q: How much should I charge for products?**
A: Research similar creators. $9-$99 is typical range.

**Q: Will discovery posts help sell products?**
A: Yes! Posts build audience trust and drive traffic to your products/IPs.

**Q: How often should I post?**
A: 2-3x weekly optimal. Consistency > quantity.

**Q: Can I edit posts after publishing?**
A: Yes, click Edit Post in the Posts tab.

**Q: What if my IP has no activity?**
A: Post about it, share in discovery, build community buzz. Quality over hype.
